Overview

Purpose

This site plan is all about creating a fun and welcoming climbing gym where everyone can enjoy the thrill of climbing, no matter their skill level. We want to build a space that encourages fitness, friendship, and a love for the outdoors, making it a go-to spot for climbers and adventurers in the community.

Audience

The audience for the climbing gym includes climbers of all levels, families, fitness enthusiasts, and local community members.

Dynamic elements

The site plan includes the development of a dynamic photo gallery, real-time class scheduling, and a dynamic pricing calculator to enhance user engagement and streamline the climbing gym experience.
